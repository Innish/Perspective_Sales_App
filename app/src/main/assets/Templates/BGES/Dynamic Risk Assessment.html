Dynamic Risk Assessment
This form is for use by competent operatives who must assess each site or task, before or as it develops and is in addition to the main risk assessment and method statement. It purpose is to identify any hazards that have not been identified. If hazards are identified they must be controlled to bring the risk As Low As Reasonable Practicable. If the risk cannot be controlled and remains high, operatives must seek advice from their supervisor before continuing.
